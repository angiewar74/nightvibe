Advancing Cancer Medicine Through Innovation

At OncoScience Research Lab, we're dedicated to developing next-generation therapeutics and personalized treatment approaches to combat cancer and improve patient outcomes worldwide.

Explore Our Research

Research Focus Areas

Our multidisciplinary team works across several key areas of cancer research to develop innovative solutions.

Targeted Therapies

Developing precision medicines that specifically target cancer cells while sparing healthy tissues, reducing side effects and improving efficacy.

Immuno-Oncology

Harnessing the power of the immune system to recognize and destroy cancer cells through novel checkpoint inhibitors and CAR-T cell therapies.

Drug Resistance

Investigating mechanisms of treatment resistance and developing strategies to overcome them for more durable cancer remissions.
